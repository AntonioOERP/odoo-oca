Invoice Stock Move

In odoo by default we cannot able to create stock from customer or vendor invoice. By installing this module, you will able to create stock directly from customer or vendor invoice.

Step 1:

By Installing this module a two configuration menu will displays in the Inventory setting.

Step 2:

By enabling this buttons, Delivery and Receive buttons will appears in the customer and vendor invoice.

Step 3:

After validating the invoice by clicking the Deliver button, a new deliver order will create against the invoice.

Step 4:

After validating the bill by clicking the Receive button, a new Shipment order will create against the bill.
